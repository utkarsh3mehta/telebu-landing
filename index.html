Frequently Asked Questions
What is TelebuSocial's WhatsApp API, and how can it benefit my business?
TelebuSocial's WhatsApp API allows you to engage with your customers directly on WhatsApp, offering personalised experiences that can positively impact your bottom line. It's designed to help you connect with your audience where they spend their time.
How do TelebuSocial's AI-driven chatbots enhance customer interaction?
Our interactive chatbot flow uses AI to guide conversations, answer frequently asked questions, and nurture leads. This helps you close deals faster and boosts your overall efficiency and customer engagement.
Can TelebuSocial help me reach a wider audience with my marketing efforts?
Yes, with TelebuSocial's Broadcast feature, you can send messages to a broader audience, enhancing your marketing campaigns and helping you onboard more customers easily.
What makes TelebuSocial's live chat feature unique?
TelebuSocial's live chat allows for meaningful two-way conversations across various social media platforms, including WhatsApp, Instagram, and Facebook. You can use our in-built CRM to manage your customer base. You can also transfer conversations from one agent/team member to another.
How does TelebuSocial integrate with third-party tools, and what are the benefits?
Our platform integrates with a Google Sheet, SalesForce, Zapier, and a variety of third-party tools to streamline your sales, marketing, and customer support processes. It's simple to set up and can significantly accelerate your business operations.
